THE ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING AND TECHNICIANS LICENSURE EXAMINATIONS

The Department of Electronics Engineering of Rizal Technological University, together with the Electronics Engineering and Technology Students’ Society (ECETSS), extends its warmest congratulations to the successful passers of the Electronics Engineer Licensure Examination (ECELE) and the Electronics Technician Licensure Examination (ECTLE).

This milestone is a reflection not only of the hard work, perseverance, and dedication of our graduates, but also of the department’s unwavering commitment to excellence in engineering education. Through rigorous training, innovative instruction, and holistic development, RTU continues to produce graduates who are ready to meet the challenges of the profession and contribute to nation-building.

Performance Results:
Electronics Engineering (ECE) — 50.00% (National Passing: 45.39%)
Electronics Technician (ECT) — 65.63% (National Passing: 81.75%)

The College of Engineering takes pride in these achievements, as they highlight the strength of RTU’s academic foundation and the resilience of its students. Our new Electronics Engineers and Electronics Technicians now carry the responsibility of applying their knowledge and skills to create solutions, foster innovation, and uphold the values of professionalism and integrity in the field of electronics.

10 years sa college, pasok sa Board Exam Top 10

"Thank you po sir! Napaka powerful po ng book nyo. Pinaka tumatak po talaga sa akin doon ang Growth Mindset. Yun po talaga ang pinanghawakan ko kaya kahit hindi po ako laude or quizzer, nagawa ko pa rin pong mag top.

Hindi po talaga ako makapaniwala na makakasampa pa ako sa top dahil 10 yrs na po ako sa college. Tumigil po ako 3 yrs para mag call center tapos pagbalik ko po sa pag aaral, na phased out naman po yung curriculum ko dahil nabago na ang curriculum sa ECE.

Kaya lahat po ng remaining subjects ko ay puro petition at tutorial. Ang hirap din po kasi may mga pagkakataon na hindi po naaapprove petition namin noon para mabuksan yung subject na pang old curriculum kaya mas lalo kaming na dedelay. Kaya wala na rin po sa bokabularyo ko ang magpaka competitive at lalo ang mag top dahil tingin ko masyado na akong napaghuhulian sa buhay.

Pero nabuhayan po ako ng loob nong mabasa ko po yung chapter na about sa aspiring topnotchers, na hindi po kailangan na maging most decorated student ako ng school ko para mag top. At dahil na din po sa The Growth Mindset, lahat po ay may kapasidad na maging magaling, na maging kasing husay ng mga nag totop sa boards.

Thank you po uli sir at sana po ay marami pa po kayong matulungan na nga future board exam takers! #otbe #SuccessStory #Topnotcher

Engr. Renair Ochale Palabasan
Rizal Technological University
Electronics Engineer (Top 10)

ACHIEVEMENTS

ATHLETICS EXCELLENCE

The Electronics Engineering Department also has student-athletes who are giving their all during College of Engineering Week and University Week. From volleyball and basketball to badminton, chess, and table tennis, our representatives showed what it truly means to play with resilience and determination. Every game was not just about winning, but about carrying the name of ECE with pride and passion.

Volleyball brought unforgettable moments of victory. During the College of Engineering Week Interdepartmental Competition, the ECE men’s team powered through every set with focus and teamwork, eventually rising as champions. The title wasn’t just a trophy; it was proof of their hard work and the unshakable bond they built throughout the journey.



View Post

And it didn’t stop there. Representing RTU on a bigger stage, our ECE volleyball athletes went on to dominate at the IECEP NCR-NESC SportsFest 2024, where they were crowned champions once again. Their victory brought pride not just to the department, but to the whole university. To make things even sweeter, several of our athletes earned individual awards, showing that ECE talent shines both as a team and as standout players.

These milestones remind us that excellence goes beyond the four walls of the classroom. Whether on the court, the field, or in competition halls, ECE student-athletes continue to prove that strength, discipline, and teamwork define who we are. Truly, the spirit of ECE lives on in every game played and every victory earned.
