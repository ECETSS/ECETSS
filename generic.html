What is ECETSS?

The Electronics Engineering Students’ Society (ECETSS) of Rizal Technological University stands as the official student organization representing all Electronics Engineering students of the College of Engineering. Rooted in the values of leadership, service, and academic excellence, ECETSS serves as a unifying body that supports the holistic development of its members.

Through initiatives such as academic reviews, technical seminars, community-building activities, and leadership training, the organization continues to foster innovation, collaboration, and resilience among students. ECETSS also upholds its mission of bridging the academic community with industry practices, ensuring that future engineers are prepared not only in theory but also in real-world applications.

The Society remains committed to guiding its members in their journey as aspiring professionals while embodying the spirit of One ECETSS — united for growth, excellence, and service.
